# Statistics & Experiments

## Overview
This folder contains projects focused on statistical analysis, experimental design,
and quantitative reasoning. The work emphasizes understanding data distributions,
testing hypotheses, and drawing valid conclusions from empirical evidence.

## Objectives
- Apply statistical methods to real-world datasets
- Formulate and test hypotheses
- Quantify uncertainty and variability
- Support data-driven decisions with statistical evidence

## Topics Covered
- Descriptive statistics
- Inferential statistics
- Hypothesis testing
- Confidence intervals
- Correlation and relationship analysis

## Methods & Techniques
- Data exploration and summary statistics
- Statistical testing and validation
- Interpretation of results and assumptions
- Clear communication of statistical findings

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Statistical libraries
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Statistical reasoning
- Experimental analysis
- Analytical rigor
- Research methodology

## Notes
Projects are documented with explanations of assumptions, methods, and conclusions
to ensure transparency and reproducibility.
